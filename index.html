ESCAPE SPACE

survive an emergency landing, with a side of espionage

Background

You and your crew were in a spaceship headed to the moon. Unfortunately, unexpected disturbances interfered with the landing sequence, and the spaceship made an emergency maneuver and landing. You are all thankfully okay, but the spaceship is in great need of repair. The ship landed on the far side of the moon, miles away from the intended landing site. After exiting the unusable spaceship, you stumble upon a habitat that appears to be abandoned.

Objective

In order to survive, the only course of action is to recover the habitat and establish communications with Mission Control. In the process of doing so, you hope to learn about the crew that used to work at this habitat, and why they were there. A quick look around indicates that the habitat is Russian. Once you have successfully contacted Mission Control, they will launch a rescue mission for you and your crew.
